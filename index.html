Pillsbury House Bed and Breakfast

At the Pillsbury House Bed and Breakfast, hospitality is not just a business, it's a way of life.Built in 1875, The Pillsbury House is tucked away on a quiet and historic Prospect Street in Woonsocket's fashionable North End. It's just a half-mile from the storied Blackstone River, the river that powered the American Industrial Revolution and is in the heart of the Blackstone River Valley National Heritage Corridor. Whether you are coming to the Blackstone Valley to visit friends or relatives, touring the historic area or traveling for business, the Pillsbury House is a home where the gentle amenities of life are still observed.
